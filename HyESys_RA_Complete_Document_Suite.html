6 Phase 6 — Work at Height / 高处作业阶段

⚠️

Work at height (>2 m) requires additional safety controls per WSH (Construction) Regulations 2007. 高处作业（高于2米）须按《2007年工作安全健康（建筑业）条例》实施额外安全措施。

StepAction / 操作Responsible
6.1Conduct work-at-height specific risk assessment before every elevated task. Identify rescue means before ascending. / 每次高处作业前进行专项风险评估，攀升前明确应急救援方案。Supervisor
6.2Preferred: use MEWP (scissor lift, boom lift) with full-body harness anchored to manufacturer's anchor point. Ensure operator is trained and certified (MEWP licence). / 首选：使用MEWP（剪叉式/臂式升降机），全身式安全带系于制造商指定锚点，操作员须持MEWP操作证。Installer (MEWP Cert.)
6.3If ladder: use EN 131 / BS 2037 rated ladder. Tie off at top. 3-point contact at all times. No ladder work above 5 m. Buddy system — standby person at base. / 如用梯子：使用EN 131/BS 2037合格梯，顶部绑扎，全程三点接触，梯子作业不超5米，底部须有人协助。Installer
6.4Erect barricades and warning signage (1.2 m barrier) around fall zone below elevated work. Remove all tools and loose objects before ascending. / 在高处作业正下方竖立护栏和警告标志（1.2米围栏），攀升前取走所有工具和松动物品。Installer
6.5All tools must be tethered or in a tool pouch. No loose items at height. Establish "Tool Drop Zone" exclusion for persons below. / 所有工具须系绳或装入工具袋，高处无散落物品，在下方人员区域设立"工具坠落禁区"。All workers
6.6Confirm rescue plan is posted at worksite and rescue equipment (rescue kit, stretcher, first aider) is accessible before commencing. / 确认救援计划张贴于现场，攀升前急救设备（救援包、担架、急救员）处于可用状态。Supervisor

🚨 Emergency Response Quick Reference / 紧急处置快速参考

  • Fire / 火灾: RACE — Rescue · Alarm · Confine · Extinguish (CO₂ only for electrical). Evacuate. Call 995.
  • Thermal Runaway / 热失控: Evacuate immediately. Exclusion 10 m. Do NOT open enclosure. Ventilate. Call 995 & SCDF.
  • Electrocution / 触电: Do NOT touch victim. Isolate MSB power. Call 995. CPR only after confirmed power OFF.
  • Fall from Height / 高处坠落: Do NOT move victim (spinal precaution). Call 995. Perform CPR/First Aid if trained. Preserve scene.
  • Chemical / Electrolyte Spill / 化学品泄漏: Ventilate area. Avoid contact. Flush affected skin/eyes ≥15 min with water. Call 995 / Poison Centre 6423-9119.
  • Emergency Contacts: SCDF 995 · Police 999 · MOM 1800-221-9922 · SP PowerGrid 1800-778-8888
